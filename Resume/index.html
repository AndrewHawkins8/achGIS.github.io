About Me

Hi, my name is Andrew Hawkins. I am enrolled at the Univeristy of Tennessee. I am majoring in Geography with a concentration in Geospatial Information Sytems (GIS). While majoring in Geography, I am minoring in Sustainability. Growing up, I loved nature. That is what pushed me to study Geography and Sustainability. As sustainabile ideas are being implemented through out cities in the US, I hope to pair my GIS skills with sustainabile ideas to create greener citys.
